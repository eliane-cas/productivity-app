How to Use This App

I found that when I felt overwhelmed by the number of tasks in a day, it really helped to plan how many Pomodoros I wanted to dedicate to each one.
Breaking tasks into 25-minute chunks made everything feel much more manageable.

So, I built this minimalist productivity tool—a simple to-do list integrated with a Pomodoro timer to help you stay focused and in control.

  1. 1. Add your tasks to the to-do list and assign the number of Pomodoros you'd like to dedicate to each.
  2. 2. The total Pomodoro counter will update automatically.
  3. 3. Use the built-in 25-minute timer to stay on track.
  4. 4. Each time you complete a Pomodoro, it updates in the total counter—no extra clicks needed.
